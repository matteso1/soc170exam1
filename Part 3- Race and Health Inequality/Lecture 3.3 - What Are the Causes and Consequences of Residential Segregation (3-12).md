# Lecture 3.3: What are the causes and consequences of residential segregation? (3/12)

Today's readings, videos, and lecture examine the history of the policies and practices that caused residential segregation and their continued influence on contemporary residential patterns. We will also consider the consequences of residential segregation on health outcomes.

## Readings

- Joe LaBriola, Housing Market Appreciation and the White-Black Wealth Gap, *Social Problems*, Volume 72, Issue 4, November 2025, Pages 1473-1491. **Read the Abstract, Introduction, and Conclusion**
- Adam Ruins Everything. "The Disturbing History of the Suburbs" video (6 minutes 19 seconds)
- Barry-Jester, A. (2016). How Americans Die May Depend on Where They Live. *FiveThirtyEight*.
- (Optional) An article on "How the Housing Boom Left Black Neighborhoods Behind". *New York Times*.
