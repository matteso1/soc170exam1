# Lecture 3.1: How do Americans think about race? (3/5)

What is race? How do we define and measure it? Where did the concept of race come from? This lecture will serve as a foundation for thinking about race sociologically.

## Readings

- Morning, Ann. (2017) "Kaleidoscope: contested identities and new forms of race membership." *Ethnic and Racial Studies*. (pp. 4-9, 13-15)
- Did The U.S. White Population Decline And Shrink? It's Complicated. *NPR*
- (Optional) Jonathan Jarry. "Are you there, Race? It's me, DNA." *McGill University* 2019.
- (Optional) Goodman, Alan. "Race is real, but it's not genetic." *Discover Magazine*. 2020.
