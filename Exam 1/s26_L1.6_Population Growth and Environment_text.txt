Why does affluence matter?
• As the consumption of individuals increases, the total 
environmental impact increases as well
•   Resources used to produce a growing number of goods
•   Waste generated in creating, transporting, and disposing of 
goods

Why is it hard to disentangle population 
growth and environmental impact?
• Role of “affluence”
• Influences both population growth and environmental impact
• More affluent have fewer children!
• The impact of poverty vs. the impact of wealth
• Poverty: environmental vulnerability
• Wealth: adaptation and consumption
