Other Data Collection Efforts in the US
(I will not test you on these!)
• The census department runs >90 surveys across many topics
• American Community Survey (ACS) is an annual version of the Census w/ 
extra info
• Current Population Survey (CPS) is a monthly version conducted by the 
Census for the Bureau of Labor Statistics, with supplements that cover 
specific topics like employment
• This is where unemployment stats come from
• Businesses, investors use these data for planning
• Economic Census (EC) is a census of businesses in the US conducted every five 
years
