# Lecture 1.5: Malthusianism and Neo-Malthusians vs. ...Elon Musk? (2/3)

Today's lecture begins with the 18th century British philosopher Malthus, who argued that population growth would eventually outpace food production, leading to a population crisis. We will discuss critiques of Malthusian theory, what demographers know about global population growth, and how population dynamics have changed over time.

## Readings

- NY Times Video (2015). The population bomb?
