What is a population?
• Empirical: Collection of people alive at a specified 
point in time who meet certain criteria
• Population of Wisconsin on July 1, 2021 ( 5,895,908 people)
• Population of UW students on September 5, 2023 (50,662)
• Conceptual: Collectivity of people that persists 
over time even though its members are always 
changing
• The “population of Wisconsin”

Better rates:
In a crude rate the denominator is the total number of people. 
But we can define the denominator more precisely!
Who is at risk of experiencing an event over this time period?
◦How many people are in the population who could actually 
experience the event? (ex: Can babies get married?)
◦What portion of the time period do they spend in the population 
before they experience the event?
