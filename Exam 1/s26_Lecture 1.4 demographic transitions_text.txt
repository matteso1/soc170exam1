Basic Components of the 2nd Demographic 
Transition (Van de Kaa 1987)
• The weakening of marriage as the only type of family 
structure, resulting from high divorce rates and a rise 
in cohabitation
• Shift in family relations from king-child with parents 
to king-couple with child
• A shift from preventive contraception to self-fulfilling 
contraception
• The uniform family (the conjugal family) starts giving 
way to more pluralistic forms of families
