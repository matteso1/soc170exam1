# Lecture 1.9: How low is US fertility? (2/17)

In most high income countries women, on average, have less than two children. What drives low fertility? How low can fertility go at the population level? Is it possible for fertility levels to be too low?

- Hartnett, C.S. and Gemmil, A. (2020) Recent Trends in U.S. Childbearing Intentions. *Demography*. 57(6) 2035-2045.
