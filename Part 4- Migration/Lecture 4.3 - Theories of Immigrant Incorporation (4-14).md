# Lecture 4.3: Theories of immigrant incorporation (4/14)

How do immigrants settle when they arrive in a new place? How do they integrate into their receiving society? How do they change their receiving society? Today's lecture will focus on prevailing sociological theories of immigrant incorporation into host societies.

## Readings

- Zhou, Min and Roberto Gonzales (2019) Divergent Destinies: Children of Immigrants Growing Up in the United States. *Annual Review of Sociology*. **Pp. 383-389**
