# Lecture 1.7: Fewer babies or later babies? (2/10)

In this lecture, we will discuss issues around the (apparent) declining fertility rate in the US. We will cover the relationship of fecundity to age, the determinants of fertility timing decisions, and will analyze trends in fertility through both period and cohort lenses.

## Readings

- Marinus J.C. Eijkemans et al. (2014) Too old to have children? Lessons from natural fertility populations. *Human Reproduction*, Volume 29, Issue 6, Pages 1304-1312. https://doi.org/10.1093/humrep/deu056
- Livingston, Gretchen. (2019) Is U.S. fertility at an all-time low? Two of three measures point to yes. *Pew Research Center*. https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2019/05/22/u-s-fertility-rate-explained/
