# Lecture 1.6: Does population growth threaten the environment? (2/5)

Today's lecture considers theories of how population growth may impact the environment. The article by Stephenson, Newman, and Mayhew considers the relationship between population dynamics and climate change. The video by Hans Rosling provides a visual illustration of the materialist perspective. As you read and watch these materials, think about how they fit together with the critiques of neo-Malthusian thinking that you read for the last lecture.

## Readings and Video

- Stephenson, Newman, and Mayhew (2010). Population dynamics and climate change: what are the links? *Journal of Public Health*. 32(2): 150-156.
- Rosling, H. (2010). The magic washing machine. TED Video (9:16)
