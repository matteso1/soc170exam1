# Lecture 1.8: Family Planning and "Unmet Need" (2/12)

Today's lecture examines the disconnect between fertility intentions and contraceptive behavior: why does unmet need for family planning persist in many parts of the globe when effective contraceptives are widely available?

## Readings

- Bongaarts, John (2014) The Impact of Family Planning Programs on Unmet Need and Demand for Family Planning. *Studies in Family Planning*. **Focus on pp. 247-252**
- Sedgh, Gilda, Lori Ashford, and Rubina Hussein. (2016) Reasons Women Cite for Not Using Contraception. *Unmet Need for Contraception in Developing Countries: Examining Women's Reasons for Not Using a Method*. **Pp. 26-30.**
