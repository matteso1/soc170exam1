# Lecture 1.4: Demographic Transitions (1/29)

In this lecture we will discuss patterns of demographic change that have occurred in countries throughout the world. Known as "demographic transitions" these patterns have important implications for the economy and society.

## Readings

- Roser, Max. (2019) Demographic transition: Why is rapid population growth a temporary phenomenon? Published online at OurWorldinData.org. https://ourworldindata.org/demographic-transition
- Lesthaeghe, R. (2014). The second demographic transition: A concise overview of its development. *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*, 111(51), 18112-18115. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1420441111 (4 pages)

## Concepts to Know

- First Demographic Transition
- Second Demographic Transition
- Dependency Ratio
- Demographic Dividend
- Maslow's theory of changing needs
- Sub-replacement fertility
