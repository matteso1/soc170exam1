# Lecture 2.2: Is population aging a problem? (2/24)

As life expectancy increases and fertility declines, the average age of the population increases, a phenomenon known as population aging. What consequences does population aging have for society? Is population aging a problem?

## Readings

- Scott, Andrew. (2018) The myth of the "aging society". *World Economic Forum*.
- Rogerson, P. (2018) America's graying population in 3 maps. *The Conversation*. May 23, 2018.
